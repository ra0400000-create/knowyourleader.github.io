KYL

Know Your Leader is a public-information platform presenting self-declared and officially available data on political candidates, sourced from government records.

About KYL

KYL (Know Your Leader) republishes publicly available information such as affidavits submitted to the Election Commission of India. KYL does not investigate, verify, or express opinions regarding guilt or innocence.

Sources & Methodology

  • Election Commission of India – Candidate affidavits
  • ADR / MyNeta (compiled from ECI affidavits)
  • Official court portals where publicly verifiable

Correction & Grievance Redressal

If any individual believes information on KYL is inaccurate or outdated, they may request correction by emailing: corrections@knowyourleader.com
